# Clip 1.1.0

Clip 1.1.0 adds optional native click highlights for clearer demonstrations.

## Click Highlights

- Enable **Click Highlights** directly from the menu-bar quick controls or in
  **Settings → Recording**.
- Clip remembers the choice and restores it when retaking a recording.
- Highlights are rendered natively into the captured video by ScreenCaptureKit.
- Cursor visibility and click highlights remain independent, and the feature
  does not require Accessibility permission.

Click Highlights are Off by default.

## Requirements and first launch

Clip requires macOS 15 or later on Apple silicon.

This build remains Apple Development signed rather than Developer ID signed and
notarized. A direct browser download may therefore require **Open Anyway** in
**System Settings → Privacy & Security**.
